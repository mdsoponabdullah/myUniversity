Understanding Vector Embeddings üî¢

Text is converted into numerical vectors that capture semantic meaning

Text Example

"What are the admission requirements?"

Vector Representation

[0.23, 0.67, 0.12, 0.89, ...]

üéØ Why Vectors?

  • Semantic similarity: Similar meanings = Similar vectors
  • Mathematical operations: Can calculate similarity scores
  • Efficient search: Fast retrieval from millions of documents
  • Language agnostic: Works across different languages
